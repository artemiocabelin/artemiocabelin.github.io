About Me

When I first joined the United States Army, I did it for the challenge and the curiosity. No one thought I could do it so I signed up. It was the best decision I've made in my life. Despite all the ups and downs that came with being in the military, I did my duty, learned the value of hard-work, and proved to the people around me what I could do.

After 2+ years in the military, I finally transitioned back into civilian life as a nurse. I worked in various healthcare settings, collaborating with other professionals from different backgrounds. It had its challenges but I enjoyed being a nurse. The 5+ years of experience I gained working in this profession instilled in me the values of teamwork, compassion, and problem-solving.

However, despite all of those experiences, nothing comes close to my love for coding. It gave me a sense of fulfillment that I felt from my previous professions when at their best. Because I wanted a way to exercise my creativity and my love for a challenge, I saw an opportunity within the world of programming. So thats how I came to be, a nurse-turned-coder specializing in full-stack web development with JavaScript, Python and Java.
